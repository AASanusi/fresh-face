Determine your skin type

Dry

  • Rarely oily
  • Feels tight and itchy
  • Tends to be flaky
  • Skin easily irritated

Oily

  • Face is shiny and greasy
  • Large and visible pores
  • Breaks out easily
  • Oily cheeks and T-zone

Combination

  • T-zone consistently oily
  • Larger pore size
  • Dry cheeks and oily in T-zone
  • Prone to acne

Acne Prone

  • Bumps on cheeks
  • Breakout easily when using new products
  • Blackheads in T-zone area
  • Whiteheads in T-zone area
