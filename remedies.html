Dry Skin Remedy

"Honey I'm Home" Face Mask

What you will need

  • Raw honey/any honey you have at home
  • Plain yogurt
  • Brush or spoon for application

How to make

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of yogurt to a bowl
  2. Mix in 1 teaspoon of honey

How to apply

  1. Using any small brush (or back of a spoon) and apply a thin layer of mixture to entire face
  2. Allow your magic mix to dry and keep on for as long as desired, we recommend at least an hour

Treatment

Yogurt contains lactic acid which is found in many high-end products. The lactic acid in the yogurt will gently exfoliate and soften the skin while Honey being natures moisturiser, will intensely hydrate the skin simultaneously.

Oily Skin Remedy

"Coffeelicious" Scrub

What you will need

  • 1 Tablespoon Ground Coffee
  • Half tablespoon Cocoa Powder
  • 3 tablespoons Aloe Vera Gel

How to make

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of ground coffee to a bowl
  2. Mix in half a tablespoon of cocoa powder
  3. Lastly, mix 3 tablespoons of aloe vera gel in the bowl

How to apply

  1. Using any small brush (or back of a spoon) and apply a thin layer of mixture to entire face
  2. Once the mixture is evenly applied to your face leave it to sit for 1 minute
  3. Then manually rubbing the scrub into your skin in circular motions
  4. Allow to sit once more until fully dry and then rinse off with cold water

Treatment

Coffee scrubs help to remove the dead skin cells which could be contributing to excess oil production. Cocoa powder helps to improve and even out skin tone which can help correct hyperpigmentation (which usually occurs alongside acne!). Combination Skin Remedy

"All(monds) you need" Combo Treatment

What you will need

  • 10 Almonds Ground
  • 1 Tablespoon Ground Oats
  • 1 Tablespoon rose water

How to make

  1. Grind 10 Almonds in a blender and add to a small bowl,
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of ground oats
  3. Then add 1 tablespoon of rose water and mix well

How to apply

  1. Using any small brush (or back of a spoon) and apply a thin layer of mixture to entire face.
  2. Allow your mixture to dry and keep on for as long as desired, we recommend at least an hour

Treatment

This treatment works great on combination skins as the properties in oats combat dry patches. Ground almonds will smooth out skin texture and allow the skin to glow. Rose water helps to overall brighten skin tone and eliminate redness.

Acne Prone Skin Remedy

"When life gives you lemons..." Spot Serum

What you will need

  • 1 whole Lemon
  • Raw Honey/ Any honey you have at home
  • A cotton bud for application

How to make

  1. Cut your lemon into a small wedge, and squeeze the juice of the lemon into a small bowl
  2. Add equal parts raw honey and combine well

How to apply

  1. Dip cotton bud into the mixture
  2. Using cotton bud apply mixture to each individual acne area

Treatment

The antibacterial qualities of the lemon will kill any bacteria and dehydrate any active acne encouraging it to shrink in size. Lemon is also packed full of vitamin c which will promote skin brightening allowing any dark marks to fade faster. The Honey is filled with peptides which allow skin soothing helping to ease any soreness, inflammation and even redness. The antioxidants present in honey also aids skin healing!
